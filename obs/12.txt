====== 12. Muuca ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-01.jpg?direct&}}

Israa-eliyuun Masriiko yadawna mangum xedeneenit kii yinin. Garuuza yakkiinim racteh, amaa Cahdi Baarho fanah adii yinin! Masriyuun lel Israa-eliyuunuh farh-isheenim dahab, burur kee aki limolem akah yoxoyin. Wuli aki diikah xiyawko gari Yallah yeemeniinih, Israa-eliyuun Masriiko tenlih yedeyin.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yalli, lellec lee wuli dherh camidda kin darur, bar lee wuli dherh camidda kin giralle yambiddileyalle ten santishe. Yalli umma gedda ten santisheyaako isheh aalih yedee. Usun aboona alle tinem lel Kayya kataanam bisa kii yine.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-03.jpg?direct&}}

Dago waqhtek sarrah, Fircawun kee kaa xiyaw sinni xasaab yisqiyyiriiniyaako Israa-eliyuun malammih ten garuuza yakkoona farhen. Usuk isheh Rummaahit inki Yalla kinam xiyaw yabloona kee Yahweh Fircawun kee kaa yallitko nabam yaarhagoona ciloh Yalli Fircawun afcaado yiytirire.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-04.jpg?direct&}}

Amaymih, Fircawun kee kaa maxaarho malammih sinni garuuza akko abitoona yeyrereddin. Israa-eliyuun Masriiti maxaarhi amiiti yublin gedda, Fircawun caskar kee Casa Bad hi fanadde yinqirqiriinim yeerhegin. Mangum mashshiiteenih lel: "Masri aymih xabne? Inkoh rabno kino!" yerhxiinih zeeren.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-05.jpg?direct&}}

Moosa Israa-eliyuunuk: "Mashshiitaanam xabanta! Kaafa Yalli sinah dhibbayeh sin gargeero kinni" ake. Amaymik sarrah yalli Moosak: "Ta xiyawah Casa Bad hi gabolle yadawna akah warish" ake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-06.jpg?direct&}}

Amaymik sarrah Yalli amaa Masriyuun Israa-eliyuun yabliinimko ciloh amaa camiddi darurta fanadde akko haye.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yalli Moosak Casa Bad hi bukalle gaba xaf-isheh laye barhisho akah warishe. Amaymik sarrah Yalli, amaa badadde arax adde yawco ciloh xaaxatti amaa badti laye gura kee mizgalle akah dufuwaakah akko abe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-08.jpg?direct&}}

Israa-eliyuun layet qaana lamma gabo akko tane kafin baarholle yeecetiinih badti addaako yedeyin. The Israelites marched through the sea on dry ground with a wall of water on either side of them.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-09.jpg?direct&}}

Amaymik sarrah Yalli amaa Masriyuun Israa-eliyuun kudak yabloona ciloh amaa darurta aganalle xaf-ishe. Masriyuun lel ten sarradde yardoona abayshen.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-10.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah usun Israa-eliyuunudde amaa araxko adde katayen, Yalli laakin Masriyuun birriigeenih ten karrossas akah yandhibbirheekah akko abe. "Kuda! Yalli Israa-eliyuunuh amxeerebii yane!" yerhxiinih zeeren.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-11.jpg?direct&}}

Israa-eliyuun inkoh nagaadde badti oocarhi wasanalle tabeenik sarrah, Yalli Moosah malammih gaba yayfaddago akah warishe. Usuk amayim abe gedda, amaa laye Masriyuun maxaarholle raddeyaako tamaa ishi awwalaahit caralle gaxte. Masriyuun maxaarhi lel inkoh yumundhucin.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-12.jpg?direct&}}

Israa-eliyuun Masriyuun rabeenim yublin gedda, Yallah yeemeniinih Moosa Yallih nabii kii yinem yeemenin.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-13.jpg?direct&}}

Yalli rabi kee garuuzinnaako ten gargeerem hi sabbatah Israa-eliyuun mango xedenih xedenen. Israa-eliyuun Masriyuun maxaarhooko ten gargeeremhi sabbatah sinni cusub xuriya simbilishoona Yallih faylah mango margaddiinon margadishen.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-14.jpg?direct&}}

Masriyuun bukalle nasre akah yoxoyeyaako garuuzinnaako ten yeyyeecem yazzakkaroona ciloh, Yalli Israa-eliyuun Faasika iggida-iggidah simbilishoona ten yeemere.  Usun lel kaamil kin ciidotta yurxudiiniyaako cadubla kin foloolih beetenih simbilishen.

//Quddus kin kitaabhi zanta: Muuca 12:33-15:21//
