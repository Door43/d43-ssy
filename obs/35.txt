====== 35. Naxrurta-le Abbahi Zanta ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-01.jpg?direct&}}

Wuli max, Yeesus mango giber yaskattit kee zambi-wannun kaa taabbo teketteya aydurisii yine.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-02.jpg?direct&}}

Diin manabottit gari amalle tineya lel Yeesus amaa zambi-wannun sacul balih ten ablii yubliniyaako sinne-sinneh kaa cadaloona erherhishen. Amaa gedda Yeesus ta zanta akah warshe: .

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-03.jpg?direct&}}

“Wuli lamma barha le xiyawti yine. Cindha kaa barhi ishi abbak: 'Abba, anu inni miiraas kado farhak aniyo' ake. Amaymih abba ishi iraaro lamma barhah xaziile.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-04.jpg?direct&}}

“Akkanih lel amaa cindha kaa barhi inkok lii yinem yesseketteh dherh baarho yedeyeyaako ishi solaadi uma xibaradde yeylee.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-05.jpg?direct&}}

“Amaymik sarrah, amaa cindha barhi alle yinelle sirii culul sayeyaako usuk macaasha akko dhaamita solaadi waye. Sarrah, bisah geyo dhiice shuqhle yibbirhe, amayim lel xasama bettiishaanam kii yine. Umaatih yine, sarrah lel mangum safriitemhi sabbatah amaa xasamat folo beeto farhe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-06.jpg?direct&}}

Abayshimalle, amaa cindha barhi ishi rooxek: 'Ayim abak aniyom?' yerhxe. Yabbah khaddamte inkoh ten dhicta macaasha lon. Anu laakin talle cululuh rabak aniyo. Edeyeyaako yabbak 'ku casbento akko farhak aniyo' akko arhxo kiyo.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-07.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah, amaa cindha barhi ishi abbah dik fanah gaxe. Usuk mirixtalle aniikoh kaa abba kaa yubleh mangum kaa raxmatishe. Ishi barha fanah yerdeh lel kaa galeeliyeyaako kaa fugute.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-08.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa barhi abbak: 'Abba, koo kee Yallih nifidde zambe abe. Anu ku barha akko yoh meedda" ake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-09.jpg?direct&}}

Kaa abba laakin ishi khaddamteeko tiyak, 'Iba lacteh aduwaaki mece sara bahteh yi barha sarish!' ake. Kaa gabadde khaatim, kaa ibadde lel kabel akah haa. Yi barhi rabeh yinemko aniinalle gaxeh yanemhi sabbatah xafla abinno kinnik ummannimko doorte ruga bahteh urxud. Usuk yeleeh yimneko kaado geytimeh yane!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-10.jpg?direct&}}

Amaymih, xiyaw xafla aboona erherhishen. Ummanimko basolle amaa naba barhi zibolle ashtuqhulii yinemko yemeete. Usuk muziqqa kee rigdo yoobbe gedda akkii tanemhi cibrah rabe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-11.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa naba barhi kaa sacal careh yemeeteh yinemhi sabbatah xafla abaa yiniinim yeerhege gedda, mangum kurrayeyaako caredde orobo xine. Kaa abba alle yewceyaako tenlih yaxtafalo kaa dhaacime hanik, buurh-ishite.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-12.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa naba barhi: 'Anu inkoh ta iggidit macna kee makkoh koh ushtuqhule! Anu, atu yoktem xineh maarhige hanik, inni kaxantoolitlih xafla akko abito walaa cindhaa lax yoh toxoyeh mitaarhige. Ku solaadi uma xibaradde yeylee ta ku barhi yemeete gedda laakin doorime ruga akah turxude!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-13.jpg?direct&}}

Abba: "Yi barhaw, atu umman gedda yoolih tanito, tamaa anu liyom lel inkoh koyyah kinni. ku sacal rabeh yimeko gaxeh yanemhi sabbatah lakain nanu xafal abinno xaqqe lino. Usuk yeleeh yimeko kado geytimeh yane!' yeh alle deheye".

//Quddus kin kitaabhi zanta: Luqqas 15:11-32//
