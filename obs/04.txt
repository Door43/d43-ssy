====== 4. Yallih wacde Abramlih ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa weecaako mango iggiditik sarralle, malammih caalamalle mango xiyaw yinin, inkoh lel inki luqhah waanishaa yinin. Yalli akah yeemereekah caalam yamagiinim hidhdhah inkille yekettiniyaako katama siraaxen.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-02.jpg?direct&}}

Mango amnonobba lii yinin, Yalli yerhxemidde lel macal lii miyiniini. Carshe aleeloona gantah lel dherh qobba siraaxoona erherhishen.Yalli lel taginah ikidde umaani abaanam katattaysheenimko, mango zambe kinam aboona dhiicaanam yuble.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-03.jpg?direct&}}

Amaymih lel, Yalli ten luqha barhsimte caynah luqhaqhalle yiybiddileyaako, amaa xiyaw umbuka caalamalle faax-ishe. Tamaa siraaxoona erherhisheenih yinin katamak lel 'Baabel' akkay yinin, tee macna lel 'amalolokka' yarhxiinim kinni. {{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-04.jpg?direct&}}

Mango iggiditik sarralle, Yalli wuli Abram akyarhxin xiyawtoolih waanishe. Yalli: "Ishi wathan kee ishi xawaz xabteyaako anu ku aybulluwe baarho fanah aduu. Anu kodde ebeerekeh lel naba dik kok abo kiyo. Ku migacak nabatiya akko abo kiyo. Koyyah dhaaco aba mara aybaarako kiyo, koyya abaara mara lel abaaro kiyo. Umbuka diika caalamalle taneya lel ku sabbatah yambaarakoona kinon" akyerhxe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-05.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa gedda Abram Yalla yoobbe. Ishi numa, Sara kee inkoh ishi khaddamte kee isheh yabbuxutem bishshiiteyaako Yalli kaa yuybulluwe baarho, Canaan akyarhiniya fanah yedee.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg?direct&}}

Abram Canaanalle yemeete gedda, Yalli: "Ishi wakalelle kulux-erhex. Ta inkoh ablii tanito baarho koo kee ku zurriyah miiraasah sinah axaw kiyo" akyerhxe. Amaymik sarrah Abram amaa baarholle sige.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg?direct&}}

Wuli lellec, Abram Malkeziidek akyarhxin nabamko naba Yallih qashshe geye. Melkeziidak Abramah dhaaco akah abeyaako: "Nabamko naba Yalli caran kee baarho leya Abramid yabaarako" yerhxe. Abram lel isheh yabbuxutemko taman-ya af akah yoxoye.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-08.jpg?direct&}}

Mango iggidit tillaabe, Abram kee Saara laakin barha lii miyiniini. Yalli Abram waanisheyaako lel barha baaho kinam kee caranalle yane xutuk balih kin zurriya yaallo kinamah malammih cahde akah saye. Abram Yallih cahdeh yeemene.Yallih cahdeh yeemenemhi sabbatah lel Yalli Abram xaqqi wanna kinam caddoyshe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg?direct&}}

Amaymik sarrah, Yalli Abramlih wacde saye. Wacde akyaanam lammaati fanadde yakke ittifaaq kinni. Yalli: "Ku rooxeeko barha koh axaw kiyo. Ku zurriyah lel Canaan baarho akah axaw kiyo" akyerhxe. Abram laakin toyendah barha lii miyine.

Quddus kin kitaabhi zanta: Erhedhdho, curfa 11-15
