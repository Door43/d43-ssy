====== 6. Yalli Isxaaqhah yaxaye ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-01.jpg?direct&}}

Abraahim mangum barrowe gedda, Isxaaq kaa barhi carsimeh labhatto yekkeh yine. Amaa gedda, Abraahim ishi khaddamteeko wulitiya amaa kaa zamad alle yinin baarho fanah ishi barha Isxaaqhah numa akah baaheh gaxo kaa dhirhiye.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-02.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa Abraahim zamad alle yinin baarho fanah dherh safarko sarrah, Yalli amaa khaddaam Ribqalle kaa baahe. Ishi lel Abraahim sacalak barhi barh√§ kii tine.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-03.jpg?direct&}}

Ribqa ishi dik xabteyaako amaa khaddaamlih Isxaaq dik fanah gaxto tirdiye. Isxaaq lel temeete geddaakoh tee marcishite.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-04.jpg?direct&}}

Dherh waqhtek sarralle, Abraahim rabe gedda, amaa Yalli inkoh kaalih sayeh yine wacdi, cahdeh kaalih abeh yineya Isxaaqhalle tillaabe. Yalli Abraahim xadde hin zurriya yaallo kinamah Yalli wacde akah sayeh yine, Isxaaq numa laakin cindham bahto mirhiicinna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-05.jpg?direct&}}

Isxaaq Ribqah dhaaco akah abe, yallih farhih lel ishi gangalle soonowa tekke. Amaa ganga, Ribqat garbadde aniikoh lel sinne-sinneh angaacii yinin. Amaymih Ribqa ayim akkii tanem Yalla esserimte.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-06.jpg?direct&}}

Yalli lel Ribqak: "Ku garbadde yane lamma barhaako lamma wathan yawcoona kinon. Sinne-sinneh lel yangaacoona kinon. Nabati lel cindhatiih khaddaam yakko kinni" akyerhxe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-07.jpg?direct&}}

Ribqat aycit yoobokin gedda, nabati casatiya kee dagor kii yooboke, migacah lel Eesaw akyerhxin. Amaymik sarrah, cindhati Eesaw sune yibbirheh yooboke, migacah lel Yacqoob akyen.
