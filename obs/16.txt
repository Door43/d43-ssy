====== 16. Gargeereenit ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-01.jpg?direct&}}

Iyyaasu rabek sarrah, Israa-eliyuun Yallah mangazzayinnooni, Canaaniyuunko ractem lel hadane kee Yallih sercat akah yaabbiiniikah akmaabinooni. Israa-eliyuun Yahweh, rummaahit Yallah, yacbudiinim hidhdhah Canaan yallitih yacbadoona erherhishen. Israa-eliyuun nugus lii miyiniinimhi sabbatah ummanti isheh mecem akkalinnannim abitak yine.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-02.jpg?direct&}}

Israa-eliyuun Yalla yaabboona xineenimhi sabbatah, Yalli ten caduwa akah ten yasciriiniikah akko abeyaako ten yiqhthsice. Ta caduwa Israa-eliyuunko nuwa akko garen, ten iraaro akko bayshen, mango mara lel akko yigdifin. Yalli akko yerhxem xineenih, sinni caduwah dhuunumeenimko mango iggiditik sarrah, Israa-eliyuun yotoobiinih ten gargeero Yalla esseren.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-03.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah Yalli amaa ten caduwaako ten yayyaaceh amaa baarholle salaam baaha gargeereena akah yoxoye. Amaymik sarrah laakin amaa xiyaw Yalla biyashshiiteenih malammih zirabti yallitih yacbadoona erherhishen. Amaymih, Yalli amaa tenah dhayelle tine, Midiyaaniyuun akyarhxin caduwa kin xiyawhi kisho akah ten yasciriinih akko aben.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-04.jpg?direct&}}

Midiyaaniyuun lel malxina iggida Israa-eliyuun ilaw inkoh bishshiiten. Israa-eliyuun mangum mashshiitenih, Midiyaaniyuun ten geyaanamko galubudde sucuten. Abayshimalle, ten gargeero Yalladde zeeren.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-05.jpg?direct&}}

Wuli max, Gidey akyarhxin Israa-eel xiyawti Midiyaaniyuun akko garacaanamko sucuteh ilaw askiyyidii yine. One day, a man of Israel named Gideon was threshing grain secretly so the Midianites would not steal it. Yallih malaykatti yemeeteh Gideyik: "Naba bahar kin Yalli koolih yane. Aduwaaki lee Midiyaaniyuunko Israa-eel garger" akye.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-06.jpg?direct&}}

Gidey abba zirabti yallah yitbirrice mazbax lii yine. Yalli Gideyih amaa mazbax yagdalo akah warishe. Gidey laakin xiyawko mashshiiteh, baarho bar takkendah ilaale. Sarrah amaa mazbax yigdileyaako dhacto-dhacto akko abe. Amaa zirabti yallih mazbax alle yinekkehi agaagalle lel cusub mazbax abeyaako Yallah sadaqha alle abe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-07.jpg?direct&}}

Malammit max amaa xiyaw amaa mazbax yiggidileyaako cayniteh yinem yublin gedda mangum kurrayen. Gidey yagdafoona lel kaa care fanah yedeyin. Kaa abba laakin: "Aymihi sinni yalla xattoona fadhdhaanam? Yalla yekkemko ishe dhawrhoo" ake. Usuk tayim yerhxemhi sabbatah lel Gidey magdafinnooni.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-08.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah Midiyaaniyuun malammih Israa-eliyuunko garacoona yemeetin. Usun lel mangat loyyiiminnixoonim kii yinin. Gidey Israa-eliyuunuh tenlih dhibbawna inkidde akah deecime. Israa-eel gargeero ciloh kaa atneefecik yanem yaythammato ciloh Gidey Yalla lamma ishaara kaa essere.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-09.jpg?direct&}}

Erherh ishaarah, Gidey ciidot anada baarholle sidisheyaako dhaxiinit asxayta aki baarholle radekkah amaa anadalle bisah raddo Yalla essere. Yalli lel mayim abe. Malammit bar lel baarho unnayteyaako amaa anada kafinih racto esserime. Israa-eliyuun Midiyaaniyuunko gargeero Yalli kayya yatnaafaco kinam lel Gidey ta lamma ishaarah yeemene.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-10.jpg?direct&}}

Israa-eliyuun caskarko 32,000 Gidey fanah yemeetinhaaki, Yalli tamari yemmengin ake. Amaymih lel Gidey dhibbaako mashshittamko 22,000 ten dik ten yedebbe. Yalli Gideyik ku labha kado gantah mangum yemmengin ake. Amaymih, Gidey 300 caskartiyaako bextih ractem inkoh ten dik ten yedebbe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-11.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa bar Yalli Gideyik: "Aduwaaki Midiyaaniyuun macaskar fanah obteh usun yarhxiinim toobbemko mashshiita racto makito" ake. Amaymih, amaa bar, Gidey amaa macaskar fanah oobeh wuli Midiyaaniyuun caskarti wuli soonoh yublem ishi zoobah warishak yoobbe. Amaa labhattih zoobatti: "Ta soonoh macna Gidey maxaarhi Midiyaaniyuun maxaarho yascaroona kinon yaanam kinni!" ake. Gidey tayim yoobbe gedda Yallah yucbude.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-12.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah Gidey ishi caskar fanah gaxeh babbarhi gashsha, kallat citro kee shiig/cambotta akah yoxoye. Amaa Midiyaaniyuun alle dhiinak yinin macaskar lel makar-ishen. Gidey 300 caskartiya Midiyaaniyuun amaa shiig ifo yabliinimko shiig citrorudde hayen.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-13.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah, Gidey caskar citror inkoh inki waqhte yigdiliniyaako, gar-heeko amaa shiighi gira faaken. Sinni gashshush yootokiinih lel: "Sef Yahweh kee Gideyih!" yeenih andhaxen.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-14.jpg?direct&}}

Yalli Midiyaaniyuun akah yamlalakkiiniikah akko abeyaako sitta yaatakoona erherhisheenih sittiya yigidifin. Akkanih lel amaa racte Israa-eliyuunuh Midiyaaniyuun yayraraddoona ten carwaako akah deecimen. Tenko mango mara akko yigdifiinih lel amaa ractem Israa-eliyuun baarhooko ten hadanen. Amaa max Midiyaaniyuunko 120,000 raben. Yalli Israa-eel gargeereh yine.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-15.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa xiyaw Gideyik nugus akko aboona farhen. Gidey amayim aboona akah masmaxinnaaki, amaa usun inkoh Midiyaaniyuunko beyeenih yinin dahab khawattim akah yaxawna ten essere.Amaa xiyaw lel Gideyih mango dahab akah yoxoyin.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-16.jpg?direct&}}

Then Gideon used the gold to make a special garment like the high priest used to wear. But the people started worshiping it as if it were an idol. So God punished Israel again because they worshiped idols. God allowed their enemies to defeat them. They finally asked God for help again, and God sent them another deliverer.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-17.jpg?direct&}}

This pattern repeated many times: the Israelites would sin, God would punish them, they would repent, and God would send a deliverer to save them. Over many years, God sent many deliverers who saved the Israelites from their enemies.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-18.jpg?direct&}}

Finally, the people asked God for a king like all the other nations had. They wanted a king who was tall and strong, and who could lead them into battle. God did not like this request, but he gave them a king just as they had asked.

//A Bible story from: Judges 1-3; 6-8//
