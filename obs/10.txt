====== 10. Tamana Bala ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-01.jpg?direct&}}

Moosa kee Haaruun Fircawun fanah yedeyin. Usun: "Israa-eel Yalli tayim yarhxe: 'Yi xiyaw yadawnaaki ten xab!'" aken. Fircawun ten mabbinna. Israa-eliyuun natsah ten dhirhiyam hidhdhah, inkinnah ziyaada yashtaqhaloona ten xaylishe!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-02.jpg?direct&}}

Fircawun ingirooko yadawna ten kalite. Amaymih lel Yalli Msarille dhacuma tamana bala alle dhirhiye. Ta balah lel Yalli Fircawun kee Masriiti yallitko xaylalem yuybulluwe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yalli umbuka Masrille acaanun alle dhirhiye. Fircawun lel ta acaanun asac-isho Moosa dhaacime. Sarraako laakin amaa acaanun bade. Fircawun lel ziyaada ishi afcaado yiytirireyaako Israa-eliyuun Masariiko yadawna akah masmaxinna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-05.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa gedda, Yalli arriima qaci bala alle dhirhiye. Amaymik sarrah qaci bala alle dhirhiye. Fircawun Moosa kee Haaruunuh akah deecimeh ta bala daw-ishisheenimko Israa-eliyuun Masriiko yawcoona kinoonim akah warishe. Moosa dhaaco abe gedda lel Yalli amaa qaca Masriiko asac-ishe. Fircawun laakin ishi afcaado yiytirireyaako, Israa-eliyiyuun natsah yadawna ten mixabinna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-06.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah, Yalli inkoh amaa Masriyuun cari bahayim laaxuuteenih akah badaanaakah akko abe. Fircawun afcaado titrireyaako, Israa-eliyuun yadawna akah masmaxinna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-07.jpg?direct&}}

Sarrah Yalli Moosak Fircawun nifidde gaxeyaako gombots caranalle xarho akah warishe. Amayim abe gedda lel Israa-eliyuunko makiiki Masriyuunko waaxelta le biyok irot qarbatalle akko yewce. Yalli Fircawun afcaado yiytirireyaako, israa-eliyuun natsah yadawna ten mixabo.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-08.jpg?direct&}}

Amaymik sarrah, Yalli mango ilaw kee irolle awcinnantiya inkoh yagdife barad dhirhiye. Fircawun Moosa kee Haaruunuh deecimeh: "Anu zambe abeh aniyo. Tadawna dhictan" ake. Amaymih Moosa dhaaco abe gedda caranko oobak yine barad daw-yerhxe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-09.jpg?direct&}}

Fircawun laakin malammih zambe abeyaako ishi afcaado yiytirire. Israa-eliyuun natsah yadwna lel ten mixabinna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-10.jpg?direct&}}

So God caused swarms of locusts to come over Egypt. These locusts ate all the crops that the hail had not destroyed.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-11.jpg?direct&}}

Then God sent darkness that lasted for three days. It was so dark that the Egyptians could not leave their houses. But there was light where the Israelites lived.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-12.jpg?direct&}}
