====== 33. Xarastaa Zanta ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-01.jpg?direct&}}

Wuli max, Yeesus mango xiyaw wuli igil afalle aydurusik yine. Yeesus yaabboona mango mari yemeetiinimhi sabbatah, tenlih akko waanisha cara geyo ciloh lel jalbadde koreyaako igil wasanakke gaxe. Jalabadde defeyeh lel amaa xiyaw yuyduruse.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yeesus ta zanta akah warishe: "Wuli xarastaa ilaw yazraw yedee. Amaa zara gabah azriik, amaa zaraako gari araxadde radeyaako kimbir yemeeteh amaa zara inkoh beete.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-03.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa zaraako gari lel fire buure alle dago dhayitidde rade. Amaa dhayitle baarholle rade zari dheh yubqhulehaaki, rimiida meceetih buuredde guballe dhirhiyo mirhiicinna. Ayro temeeteh lacte gedda lel amaa buqhli caamuuleyaako xarare.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-04.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa zaraako wulim lel xorhudde rade. Amaa zari carsimo erherhishehaaki keenan kaa yisniqhe. Amaa keenanle baarhodde yubqhule zari lel walaa inki fire baaho mirhiicinna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-05.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa zaraako gari mece buuredde rade. Ta zari lel 30, 60, inkinnah 100 waqhtiya amaa yimzirriyeh yine zaraako ziyaada sasane. Cokka leti yaabboo!"

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-06.jpg?direct&}}

Amaa darasa ta zanta darasa ten teylelekke. Amaa gedda Yeesus taginah yishrixe: "Amaa zari Yallih waani kinni. Amaa arax lel Yallih waani yaabbe xiyawto kinnihaaki miyafhime, sheethan lel amaa waani akko beya. This story confused the disciples. So Jesus explained, “The seed is the word of God. The path is a person who hears God’s word, but does not understand it, and the devil takes the word away from him.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-07.jpg?direct&}}

“The rocky ground is a person who hears God’s word and accepts it with joy. But when he experiences hardship or persecution, he falls away.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-08.jpg?direct&}}

“The thorny ground is a person who hears God’s word, but as time passes, the cares, riches, and pleasures of life choke out his love for God. As a result, the teaching he heard does not produce fruit.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-09.jpg?direct&}}

“But the good soil is a person who hears the word of God, believes it, and produces fruit.”

//A Bible story from: Matthew 13:1-8, 18-23; Mark 4:1-8, 13-20; Luke 8:4-15//
