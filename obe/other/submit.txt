====== submit ======

===== Definition: =====

To voluntarily place yourself under the authority of a person or government.

  * The New Testament states that believers are to submit themselves to the Lord Jesus Christ.
  * As believers in Christ we are to submit ourselves to the government of our land; with the exception of those laws which would be plainly contrary to God's commands for us.
