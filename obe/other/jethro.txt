====== Jethro ======

===== Facts: =====

Jethro was the father of Moses' wife, Zipporah.

  * When Moses was a shepherd in the land of Midian, he met a man named Jethro, whose daughters were watering their sheep at a well there.
  * Moses married Zipporah, one of Jethro's daughters.
  * Many years later, Jethro met up with Moses as the Israelites were moving from place to place through the wilderness.
  * Jethro gave Moses good advice about how to get others to help with the task of judging the affairs of the people.
